Icon Requirements:

1. Create the following icons in Bitcoin orange (#f7931a) with a minimal, clean design:
   - icon16.png (16x16 pixels)
   - icon48.png (48x48 pixels)
   - icon128.png (128x128 pixels)

2. Suggested design:
   - A simple "S" with a Bitcoin symbol or a combination of a dollar sign and Bitcoin symbol
   - Use a flat design style with clean lines
   - Keep it recognizable even at small sizes

3. Tools to create icons:
   - Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, or similar graphics software
   - Online tools like Figma, Canva, or GIMP
   - Icon generators specifically for browser extensions

Remember to export in PNG format with transparency.
